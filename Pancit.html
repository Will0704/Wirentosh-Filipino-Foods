Pancit


Pancit with rice noodles, meat, shrimp, and vegetables is sure to be a family favorite. Perfect for everyday meals and special occasions!

Ingredients


1 onion, chopped
15 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup calamansi juice
1/4 cup fish sauce (patis)
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon brown sugar
1 whole chicken, cleaned and patted dry
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
6 lemon grass stalks, or more to taste
3 bay leaves
