Beef Steak


This simple yet great tasting recipe dos not require much ingredients and the procedure is not complicated at all. Just have all the ingredients present, add a little love to it and Viola! You have your dliciously cooked beef steak.

Ingredients


1 lb beef sirlion, thinly sliced
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 pc. lemon or 3 calamansi
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
3 clove garlic, crushed
1 large onion, sliced into rings
3 tbsp. cooking oil
1 salt to taste
