Guyabao Juice


Commercially-prepared guyabano juice are widely available at Asian supermarkets in my area. They are reasonably priced at about .80 cents per 8-ounce can and taste-wise, are pretty satisfactory. But if you ever had the pleasure of homemade guyabano juice, you know there is no comparison.

Ingredients


1 large (about 1 pound) soft yet firm guyabano fruit
3 cups water
2 tablespoons sugar
juice of 1 lime
