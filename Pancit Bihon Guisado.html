Pancit Bihon Guisado


Pancit Bihon Guisado with Rice noodles, meat, and vegetables is sure to be a family favorite. Perfect for everyday meals and special occasions!

Ingredients


1 onion, chopped
15 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup calamansi juice
1/4 cup fish sauce (patis)
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon brown sugar
meat
rice noodles
vegetables
