Lechon Manok


Lechon Manok or Spit-roasted Chickenis a Filipino Favorite. It goes well with our homemade Lechon Manok sauce and some papaya atchara on the side. You will enjoy eating this with freshly cooked white rice.

Ingredients


1 onion, chopped
15 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup calamansi juice
1/4 cup fish sauce (patis)
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 taespoon brown sugar
1 whole cicken, cleaned and patted dry
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
6 lemon grass stalks, or more to taste
3 bay leaves
