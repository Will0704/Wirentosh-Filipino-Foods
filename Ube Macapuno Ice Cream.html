Ube Macapuno Ice Cream


Ube Macapuno Ice Cream made with purple yam and coconut sport. Rich, creamy, and with intense ube flavor, it’s your next favorite frozen treat! It’s so easy to make, only 4 ingredients and no ice cream maker needed!

Ingredients


2 cups ube halaya.
1 cup macapuno or young coconut flesh cut into strings.
2 cups heavy cream.
2 cups fresh whole milk (evaporated milk is fine if you don't have fresh milk)
2 tsps vanilla extract.
1 cup sweetened condensed milk (add more or less depending on how sweet your ube halaya is)
