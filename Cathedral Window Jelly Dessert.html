Cathedral Window Jelly Dessert


Cathedral Window Gelatin is show-stopping dessert masterpiece perfect for the holidays. Soft, creamy and silky with colorful gelatin, it’s as amazing as it looks!

Ingredients


½ cup pineapple juice
½ cup condensed milk
½ cup heavy cream
4 tbsp sugar
¼ tsp salt
1 ½ cups water
3 tbsp unflavoured gelatin
1 cup boiling water
