Buko Salad Drink


Buko salad drink made with young coconut, gelatin, sago, and palm fruits. Sweet and creamy, it’s the perfect summer refreshment!

Ingredients


2 bars (.70 ounce) red agar-agar
3 cups water
1 cup sugar
1 bottle (12 ounces) kaong
1 bottle (12 ounces) nata de coco
1 bottle (12 ounces) cooked sago (check here for how to cook sago)
2 cups young coconut, shredded
2 cups coconut milk
4 cups coconut juice
1 (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
2 drops red food coloring , optional
